## Intent
Turn the restaurant's full food and bar menus into structured, typed content and present them in a fast, filterable, mobile-friendly experience. Data is shaped so it could later back a cart/DB (forward-compatibility).

## Features
- `FEAT-menu-1` — Menu content model & seeded data
- `FEAT-menu-2` — Visual filterable food menu page
- `FEAT-menu-3` — From the Bar page
