## Intent
Convert visitors into guests: a reservation request form, a catering inquiry, a photo gallery for atmosphere, and social proof via testimonials. All forms route through the swappable `submit()` seam so a future API/DB can replace third-party delivery without UI changes.

## Features
- `FEAT-engagement-1` — Reservation request form
- `FEAT-engagement-2` — Catering page & inquiry form
- `FEAT-engagement-3` — Photo gallery
- `FEAT-engagement-4` — Testimonials
